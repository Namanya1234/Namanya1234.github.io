About this page:

We are presenting our findings regarding the Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN) survey. There will four pictures of the heatmaps and eight pictures for screenshots from the application that we have used, Netspot.

The location of our survey is done in Rio's house, which has a 15.2 m x 14.95 m area. The WiFi access points are all 5 GHz WiFi 5, one is the house's only router and the other two are our phone's mobile hotspot. The middle access point is the router, the right access point is Rio's phone, and the left access point is Rafa's phone.
